# Nutrition for Skin and Hair: Complete Diet Guide 2026

What you eat directly affects your appearance. This guide covers optimal nutrition for skin and hair.

## Best Foods for Skin

### Fatty Fish (Salmon, Mackerel)

- Omega-3s reduce inflammation
- Improves hydration
- 2-3 servings/week

### Avocados

- Healthy fats for skin elasticity
- Vitamin E for protection

### Berries

- Antioxidants prevent aging
- Vitamin C for collagen

### Eggs

- Biotin for hair/skin
- Protein for structure

### Sweet Potatoes

- Vitamin A protects from sun
- Beta-carotene for glow

## Foods to Avoid

- **Dairy:** Linked to acne in many people
- **Sugar:** Causes glycation, accelerates aging
- **Processed foods:** Inflammation
- **Alcohol:** Dehydrates skin

## Best Foods for Hair

- Eggs (protein, biotin)
- Spinach (iron, vitamins)
- Salmon (omega-3)
- Nuts (zinc, vitamin E)
- Greek yogurt (protein)

## Daily Diet Template

**Breakfast:** Eggs + avocado + berries
**Lunch:** Salmon + vegetables + sweet potato  
**Dinner:** Lean protein + greens + healthy fats
**Snacks:** Nuts, Greek yogurt

**Hydration:** 80-100 oz water

**Cost:** Similar to regular diet if meal prepping
**Impact:** +0.2 to +0.4 skin/hair improvement over months
