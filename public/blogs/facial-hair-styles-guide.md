# Facial Hair Styles Guide: What Works for Your Face 2026

Facial hair can improve your face rating by +0.3 to +0.7 points if done right. This guide covers which styles work for each face shape and how to maintain them.

## Facial Hair Impact on Attractiveness

**Well-Groomed Beard:** +0.3 to +0.7 points
**Patchy/Unkempt:** -0.2 to -0.4 points  
**Clean-Shaven:** Baseline (0 points)

**The Truth:** Beards enhance jawlines and masculinity when done properly.

## Best Facial Hair by Face Shape

### Oval Face (Balanced)

**Best Styles:** Any style works
**Recommended:** Full beard, stubble, goatee all look good

### Round Face (Needs Angularity)

**Best:** Squared-off full beard (adds angles)
**Avoid:** Round goatee (emphasizes roundness)

### Square Face (Already Angular)

**Best:** Stubble, short full beard  
**Avoid:** Very long beard (too harsh)

### Oblong/Long Face

**Best:** Wide mutton chops, full beard (adds width)
**Avoid:** Goatee (lengthens face more)

### Diamond Face

**Best:** Full beard (balanced)
**Avoid:** Soul patch alone

### Triangle/Pear (Narrow Forehead)

**Best:** Mustache + beard (balances top)

## Beard Grooming Essentials

**Tools Needed:**

- Beard trimmer ($30-60)
- Scissors
- Beard oil ($10-15)
- Beard brush ($8-12)

**Daily Routine:**

- Wash with beard shampoo
- Apply beard oil
- Brush/comb

**Weekly:**

- Trim neckline (2 fingers above Adam's apple)
- Shape cheek line
- Trim length

**Cost:** $50-100 setup, $20/month maintenance

## Growing a Beard from Scratch

**Month 1:** Awkward phase, resist urge to trim
**Month 2-3:** Shape emerges, start trimming neckline
**Month 4+:** Full beard, maintain regularly

**If Patchy:** Minoxidil can fill gaps (see minoxidil beard guide)

## Stubble vs Full Beard

**Stubble (3-5mm):**

- Low maintenance
- Masculine, rugged
- Works for most

**Short Beard (10-20mm):**

- Defines jaw without hiding it
- Professional
- Sweet spot for many

**Long Beard (30mm+):**

- Statement piece  
- Higher maintenance
- Can hide weak jaw (good or bad)

## Common Facial Hair Mistakes

**❌ Unkempt Neckline:** Makes beard look sloppy
**❌ Patchy Beard:** Either fix with minoxidil or stay clean-shaven  
**❌ Wrong Style for Face:** Lengthens when you need width, etc.

## Your Facial Hair Action Plan

**Week 1:** Let beard grow, no trimming
**Week 4:** Define neckline and cheek lines
**Month 3:** Assess fullness, decide on style
**Ongoing:** Maintain with regular trimming

**Impact:** +0.3 to +0.7 face rating if executed well
