# Grooming Routine for Men: Complete Daily Guide 2026

A complete grooming routine takes 15-20 minutes daily and improves your face rating by +0.5 to +1.2 points. This is the complete protocol.

## Morning Routine (10 minutes)

**Facial Care (5 min):**

1. Cleanser (CeraVe Foaming, $9)
2. Vitamin C serum ($16)
3. Moisturizer
4. Sunscreen SPF 50 ($17)

**Hair (3 min):**

1. Style with product (matte clay/pomade)
2. Check for stray hairs

**Facial Hair (2 min if applicable):**

1. Quick trim of neckline
2. Beard oil application

**Cost:** $50/month

## Evening Routine (10 minutes)

**Facial Care (7 min):**

1. Cleanser
2. Tretinoin or retinol (3x/week)
3. Moisturizer

**Dental (3 min):**

1. Brush 2 minutes
2. Floss
3. Mouthwash

**Cost:** $40/month

## Weekly Maintenance (30 min)

**Sunday Grooming Session:**

- Detailed facial hair shaping
- Eyebrow tweezing (unibrow removal)
- Nose/ear hair trimming
- Exfoliation (2x/week)

## Monthly Tasks

- Haircut ($20-40)
- Beard professional trim (if growing long)
- Replace worn grooming tools

## Common Grooming Mistakes

**❌Skip**ping sunscreen (biggest mistake)
**❌ Overwashing** face (strips oils)
**❌ Using bar soap** on face (too harsh)
**❌ Neglecting eyebrows** (instant unkempt look)
