# Complete Looksmaxxing Transformation Timeline: 0-12 Months

This is the exact month-by-month protocol to transform from average to significantly above average in 12 months.

## Month 1: Foundation & Assessment

**Week 1:**

- Get baseline face rating (looksmaxxking.com)
- Take progress photos (multiple angles)
- Body composition assessment

**Week 2-4:**

- Start skincare routine (cleanser, moisturizer, SPF)
- Begin gym program (3x/week)
- Fix diet (calorie deficit if >15% BF)

**Investment:** $100 (skincare + gym)
**Results:** Minimal visible change yet

## Month 2-3: Habit Formation

**Daily:**

- AM/PM skincare consistent
- Gym 4x/week
- Track calories

**New Additions:**

- Add retinol (PM)
- Start mewing practice
- Eyebrow grooming weekly

**Investment:** $50/month ongoing
**Results:** Skin improving, body fat down 2-4%

## Month 4-6: Visible Transformation

**Upgrades:**

- Tretinoin prescription (upgraded retinol)
- New wardrobe (fitted clothes)
- Professional haircut/style

**Physical:**

- 10-15 lbs fat loss OR muscle gain
- Jawline emerging
- Skin clearer

**Investment:** $200 (wardrobe) + $80/month
**Results:** +0.3 to +0.6 face rating improvement

## Month 7-9: Refinement

**Add:**

- Dermarolling (microneedling)
- Advanced grooming (beard oil, styling)
- Posture fully corrected

**Consider:**

- Minoxidil (if beard/hair needed)
- Teeth whitening
- Professional skin treatments

**Investment:** $100/month
**Results:** +0.5 to +0.9 total improvement

## Month 10-12: Optimization

**Finals:**

- Body composition goal hit (10-12% BF or +20 lbs muscle)
- Skin optimized (tretinoin working)
- Style/grooming mastered

**Optional Enhancements:**

- Professional photos
- Dating profile optimization
- Consider minor procedures (filler, etc.) if needed

**Investment:** $120/month + optional procedures
**Results:** +0.7 to +1.5 total face rating improvement
