# Body Language and Attractiveness: Complete Guide 2026

Body language accounts for 35% of first impressions. This guide shows how to project attractiveness through non-verbal communication.

## Attractive Body Language

### Posture

- Stand tall, shoulders back
- Head level, chin slightly raised
- Open stance (not crossed arms)

### Eye Contact

- Hold 3-5 seconds before looking away
- Look at eyes, not down
- Soft gaze, not staring

### Movement

- Move slowly and deliberately
- No fidgeting
- Take up space confidently

### Facial Expressions

- Slight smile (not grimacing)
- Relaxed face
- Engaged expressions when listening

## Unattractive Body Language

❌ Slouching
❌ Crossed arms (defensive)
❌ Looking down/avoiding eye contact
❌ Fidgeting/touching face
❌ Taking up minimal space
❌ Rushed movements

## Quick Fixes

**Before Important Moments:**

- Power pose (2 min alone) - reduces cortisol
- Deep breaths
- Shoulders back, chest out
- Slight smile

**During Interactions:**

- Mirror the other person subtly
- Lean in slightly when listening
- Nod to show engagement
- Keep hands visible
