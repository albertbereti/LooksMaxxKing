# Anti-Aging Guide for Men: Prevention Protocol 2026

Start anti-aging in your 20s-30s for best results. This guide covers proven prevention strategies.

## The Big 3 of Anti-Aging

### 1. Sunscreen SPF 30-50 Daily

**Why:** 80% of facial aging is sun damage
**Cost:** $15-40/month
**Impact:** Prevents 80% of wrinkles, spots, sagging

### 2. Tretinoin (Vitamin A)

**Why:** Only proven topical for wrinkle reversal
**Dose:** 0.025-0.1% nightly
**Cost:** $20-50/month
**Impact:** Increases collagen, cell turnover, prevents wrinkles

### 3. Healthy Lifestyle

- Sleep 7-9 hours
- No smoking
- Limited alcohol
- Exercise
- Hydration

## Anti-Aging Timeline by Age
