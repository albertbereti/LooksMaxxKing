## Application Tips

**Where:** Pulse points (wrists, neck, behind ears)
**How Much:** 2-4 sprays max
**Distance:** 6 inches from skin
**When:** After shower on dry skin

**Don't:**

- Rub wrists together (breaks molecules)
- Over-apply (3 feet radius max)
- Spray on clothes
