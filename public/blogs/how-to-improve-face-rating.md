## The 10-Step Face Rating Improvement Protocol

### 1. Reduce Body Fat (Biggest Impact: +0.5 to +2 points)

**Why it works:** Lower body fat reveals your bone structure—jaw definition, cheekbones, and facial angles become visible.

**How to do it:**

- Target 10-15% body fat for men, 18-22% for women
- Caloric deficit of 300-500 calories/day
- Prioritize protein (0.8-1g per lb bodyweight)
- Resistance training 3-4x/week

**Timeline:** 3-6 months for significant facial changes

### 2. Perfect Your Skincare (+0.5 to +1 point)

Clear, glowing skin dramatically improves attractiveness.

**Morning routine:**

1. Gentle cleanser
2. Vitamin C serum
3. Moisturizer with SPF 30+

**Evening routine:**

1. Cleanser
2. Retinol (start 2x/week, build to nightly)
3. Hydrating moisturizer

**Pro tip:** Add chemical exfoliant (AHA/BHA) 2x/week

### 3. Get the Right Haircut (+0.3 to +0.7 points)

Your hairstyle should complement your face shape:

- **Oval face:** Most styles work - experiment freely
- **Round face:** Add height on top, short on sides
- **Square face:** Textured top, softer sides
- **Oblong face:** Add width, avoid excess height
- **Heart-shaped:** Medium length with side volume

### 4. Start Mewing (+0.2 to +0.8 points over time)

**What is mewing?** Proper tongue posture that may improve facial structure over months/years.

**How to mew:**

1. Close your mouth
2. Press entire tongue (including back) against roof of mouth
3. Teeth lightly touching or slightly apart
4. Maintain this 24/7 (except when eating/talking)

**Results timeline:** 6-24 months for noticeable changes (younger = faster)

### 5. Groom Your Eyebrows (+0.2 to +0.4 points)

Well-groomed eyebrows frame your face and eyes.

**For men:**

- Clean up unibrow
- Trim overly long hairs
- Remove stragglers below brow line
- Keep natural thickness

**For women:**

- Professional shaping recommended
- Maintain natural arch
- Fill sparse areas with brow pencil

### 6. Fix Your Teeth (+0.3 to +0.6 points)

Straight, white teeth significantly boost attractiveness.

**Quick wins:**

- Professional whitening treatment
- At-home whitening strips
- Brush 2x daily, floss daily
  
**Bigger improvements:**

- Clear aligners (Invisalign) for crooked teeth
- Veneers for major corrections (expensive but transformative)

### 7. Improve Your Jawline (+0.3 to +1 point)

**Non-surgical methods:**

- Lose facial fat (see step 1)
- Chew harder foods (activate masseter muscles)
- Mewing for forward growth
- Neck exercises for definition

**Advanced:** Jaw exercisers like Jawzrsize (use cautiously - can cause TMJ issues)

### 8. Master Under-Eye Care (+0.2 to +0.5 points)

Dark circles and bags make you look tired and older.

**Solutions:**

- Quality sleep (7-9 hours)
- Caffeine eye cream
- Cold compress in morning
- Hydration (drink more water)
- Reduce salt intake

**Advanced:** Under-eye filler by professional (temporary, $500-1000)

### 9. Optimize Your Style (+0.3 to +0.5 points)

Your clothing affects face perception.

**Key principles:**

- Well-fitted clothes (not baggy or too tight)
- Colors that complement your skin tone
- Clean, maintained wardrobe
- Appropriate for your age/context

### 10. Fix Your Posture (+0.2 to +0.3 points)

Good posture improves facial structure presentation.

**Posture checklist:**

- Shoulders back and down
- Chin slightly tucked (not forward)
- Chest up
- Core engaged

**Exercises:**

- Face pulls (gym)
- Wall angels
- Stretching tight chest muscles
