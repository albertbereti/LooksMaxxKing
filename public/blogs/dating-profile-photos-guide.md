Your dating profile photo is your first impression. This guide shows exactly how to take photos that maximize matches.

## Photo Quality Impact

**Professional-Quality Photos:** 3-5x more matches
**Mediocre Phone Selfies:** Baseline
**Bad Lighting/Angles:** -50% to -70% matches

**The Truth:** Same person, different photos = 300-500% difference in results

## The Perfect Dating Profile Photo Formula

### Photo 1: Face Close-Up (Primary)

**Requirements:**

- Natural lighting (golden hour or soft window light)
- Eye level or slightly below
- Genuine smile (or serious/confident expression)
- Sharp focus on eyes
- Clean background (solid or blurred)

**Avoid:**

- Selfies (use timer or friend)
- Bathroom/gym mirror pics
- Sunglasses hiding eyes
- Poor lighting (overhead fluorescent)

### Photo 2: Full Body

**Shows:**

- Height, build, proportions
- Fashion sense
- Lifestyle context

**Best Settings:**

- Outdoor location
- Doing activity (hiking, event, etc.)
- Well-fitted clothes

### Photo 3-4: Lifestyle/Hobby

- Shows personality
- Demonstrates interesting life
- Socializing (with other people)

## Technical Photo Settings

**Camera:**

- DSLR or mirrorphone camera (portrait mode)
- Not front-facing selfie camera (distorts face)

**Settings:**

- Slight distance (5-8 feet, optical zoom)
- Portrait mode (blurred background)
- Eye-level angle or 10° below

**Lighting:**

- Golden hour (hour after sunrise, before sunset)
- Overcast day (soft, even light)
- Window light indoors

**Edit:**

- Slight brightness/contrast adjustment
- NO heavy filters (catfishing)
- Color correction only

## Common Photo Mistakes

**❌ Group Photos as Primary:** Can't tell which one you are
**❌ Snapchat Filters:** Immediately swipe left
**❌ Shirtless Mirror Selfie:** Try-hard unless very fit
**❌ All Selfies:** Looks like you have no friends
**❌ Old Photos (>1 year):** Catfishing

## Photo Checklist

✅ 4-6 total photos
✅ At least 1 clear face close-up  
✅ At least 1 full body
✅ Natural lighting in most
✅ Genuine smile in at least one
✅ Variety (not all same setting)
✅ High resolution (not pixelated)
✅ Recent (within 6 months)
