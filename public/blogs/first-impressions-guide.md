# First Impressions Guide: Psychology of Attractiveness 2026

First impressions are made in 7 seconds. This guide shows how to maximize that critical window.

## The 7-Second Rule

**What Gets Judged:**

- Face (40%)
- Body language (35%)
- Grooming/Style (20%)
- Voice (5% - if speaking)

## Optimizing Each Factor

### Face (Maximize Existing Features)

- Good skincare = healthier appearance
- Groomed eyebrows/facial hair
- Confident expression

### Body Language (Free Improvement)

- Stand tall (posture)
- Shoulders back
- Eye contact
- Genuine smile

### Grooming/Style

- Clean, fitted clothes
- Well-groomed hair
- Fresh scent (light cologne)
- Clean nails

### Voice (If Speaking)

- Speak slower than you think
- Lower pitch from diaphragm
- Clear enunciation

## First Impression Checklist

Before any important meeting/date:

- ✅ Hair styled
- ✅ Face clean, moisturized
- ✅ Breath fresh
- ✅ Clothes lint-free, fitted
- ✅ Shoes clean
- ✅ Light fragrance
- ✅ Confident posture

**Impact:** Maximizes first 7 seconds = better outcomes in dating, career, social
